Tiling FAQ

Common questions regarding our premium tiling services.

What types of tiles do you install for luxury residences?
We install high-end porcelain, polished ceramic, natural marble, granite, and specialized glass mosaic tiles for swimming pools and feature walls.
Do you provide surface leveling and waterproofing before tiling?
Yes, proper substrate preparation is critical. We perform laser screed leveling and apply premium waterproofing membranes before laying tiles, ensuring a perfectly flat, crack-free finish.
Can you handle high-traffic commercial flooring?
Absolutely. We select and install heavy-duty, slip-resistant commercial tiles designed to withstand heavy foot traffic in shopping malls, office lobbies, and public corridors.
