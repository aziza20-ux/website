Electrical Services FAQ

Common questions regarding our electrical engineering contracting.

Can you design and install heavy-duty industrial electrical panels?
Yes, we custom engineer, assemble, and install Main Distribution Boards (MDB), Sub-Main Distribution Boards (SMDB), and Motor Control Centers (MCC) for commercial and industrial facilities.
Do you provide backup power generator and solar inverter installations?
Absolutely. We supply, install, and integrate automated diesel generators, ATS panels, and hybrid solar inverter systems to ensure uninterrupted power supply for your property.
Are your electrical installations tested for earthing and lightning protection?
Yes, comprehensive earthing grid testing and lightning protection installation are standard protocols in all our electrical engineering projects to ensure absolute structural safety.
