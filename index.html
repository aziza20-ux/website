Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and contracting process.

What areas in Kigali do you serve?
We operate across all districts of Kigali, including Gasabo, Kicukiro, and Nyarugenge, providing rapid response for both commercial and residential projects, We even operate out of kigali city to other parts of the country on request for large projects.
Do you handle large commercial installations?
Yes, we specialize in large-scale commercial plumbing, electrical engineering, fire fighting systems, and structural waterproofing for high-rises, shopping malls, and housing estates.
How can I get an estimate for my project?
You can reach out to us via phone, WhatsApp, or email. We typically conduct a professional site visit to assess your exact requirements and provide a precise, transparent quotation.
Are your engineers and technicians certified?
Absolutely. All our engineers, plumbers, and electricians hold relevant professional certifications and undergo continuous training on modern building codes and safety standards.
