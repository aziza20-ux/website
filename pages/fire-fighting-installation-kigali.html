Fire Protection FAQ

Common questions regarding our fire fighting installations.

Do your fire fighting installations comply with Rwandan safety regulations?
Yes, all our fire protection designs and installations strictly adhere to the Rwanda Building Code and NFPA international standards, ensuring complete regulatory compliance.
Can you install fire suppression systems in server rooms and data centers?
Absolutely. We install clean-agent gas suppression systems (such as FM-200 and Novec 1230) that rapidly extinguish fires without damaging sensitive electronic equipment.
Do you provide routine maintenance and testing for fire pumps and hydrants?
Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance contracts to conduct periodic pressure testing, flow verification, and servicing of fire pumps, hydrants, and sprinkler heads.
