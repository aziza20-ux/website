Specialized Waterproofing Solutions

We deploy cutting-edge chemical and membrane technologies to ensure absolute structural impermeability.

Foundation & Basement Sealing

Positive and negative side waterproofing for subterranean structures, basements, and lift shafts using crystalline slurries and robust sheet membranes.

Flat Roof & Terrace Waterproofing

Application of APP modified bitumen torch-on membranes, liquid polyurethane coatings, and UV-reflective sealers for concrete rooftops and exposed balconies.

Retaining Wall Damp-Proofing

Comprehensive damp-proofing and sub-soil drainage installation behind retaining walls to relieve hydrostatic pressure and eliminate lateral moisture penetration.

Water Tank & Pool Lining

Non-toxic, potable-water-safe cementitious waterproofing coatings for underground concrete water reservoirs, overhead tanks, and swimming pool shells.

Waterproofing FAQ

Common questions regarding structural damp-proofing and sealing.

What waterproofing methods do you use for concrete flat roofs?
We utilize APP modified bitumen torch-on membranes, liquid applied polyurethane coatings, and crystalline waterproofing compounds depending on the structural load and exposure.
Can you fix existing basement leaks and rising damp?
Yes, we specialize in remedial waterproofing. We perform high-pressure chemical injection, negative side crystalline slurry application, and exterior drainage correction to eliminate rising damp.
Do you provide a warranty on your waterproofing installations?
Absolutely. All our structural waterproofing installations come with a comprehensive, long-term workmanship and material warranty for total peace of mind.
